Fudge Puddles

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cupp milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can sweetened condesensed milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup pecan halves

Directions

  1. Stir together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Cream Butter, peanut butter, and white and brown sugars.
  3. Mix in egg and ½ teaspoon vanilla.
  4. Stir flour mixture into creamed mixture.
  5. Shape dough into 48 balls, place each ball in on compartment of a muffin tin.
  6. Put chocolate chips in a double broiler over simmering water, mixing in milk and vanilla.
  7. Bake in the oven for 14 to 16 minutes. Remove and make wells in the center using a melon baller.
  8. Cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then remove to wire racks
  9. Using a measuring cup, fill each shell with cholocate mixture. Top with pecan half.
