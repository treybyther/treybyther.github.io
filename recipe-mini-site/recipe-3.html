Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 10 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon corn starch
  • ¾ cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows
  • 1 bar Hershey's chocolate
  • 4 Graham crackers

Directions

  1. Chop Graham crackers and Hershey bar.
    2. >
  2. Combine flour, salt, baking powder, baking soda, and cornstarch in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter and sugars in a bowl until creamy. Add in egg and vanilla.
  4. Gradually add flour mixture to butter mixture, beating on low speed.
  5. Add half of the marshmallows, chocolate chips, and Graham crackers to the dough.
  6. Refrigerate dough for an hour.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes. Top cookies with extra marshmallow, and bake for 2-3 more minutes.
