Snickerdoodle Cookies

A scrumptious Snickerdoodle cookie. Coated with tons of cinnamon and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Dough Ingredients
    • 1 cup unsalted butter
    • 1.5 cups sugar
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla
    • 2.75 cups flour
    • 1.5 teaspoons cream of tartar
    • 0.5 teaspoon baking soda
    • 0.75 teaspoon salt
  • Cinnamon-Sugar Mix
    • 0.25 cups sugar
    • 1.5 tablespoons cinnamon

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar for 4-5 minutes. Look for light and fluffy.
  3. Add in eggs and vanilla, then cream for 1-2 minutes more.
  4. Stir in flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt.
  5. In a small separate bowl, stir together sugar and cinnamon.
  6. Roll dough into small balls (roughly one "spoonful" large).
  7. Drop ball into sugar and cinnamon mixture and coat well.
  8. Add extra cinnamon and sugar to liking.
  9. Place on parchment paper-lined baking sheet.
  10. Bake for 9-11 minutes.
