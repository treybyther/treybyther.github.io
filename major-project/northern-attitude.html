Facts

Album release: October 14, 2022

Run time: 55 minutes, 28 seconds

Album Thoughts

This is his most recent album, and also his best album to date. This is what launched Kahan's career, with the song Stick Season reaching 1 billion streams on Spotfiy. This is a no-skip album, where Kahan has seriously settled into his sound and created a name for himself. He did end up releasing a second version of this album, however it was all the same songs with features on them, so I decided not to include it.

Favorite Song

The View Between Villages

All 3 of my top favorite songs come from this album, but The View Between Villages really stands out amongst the rest for me. It is one of my most streamed songs of all time, and it's because not only does he allow you to connect to it on a deep level lyrically, but there a big moments instrumentally that really make you feel.
