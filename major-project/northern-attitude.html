Reasoning

This song is slightly different than the one on the album, as it features Hozier on it. It is probably my favorite collab to date. This song is perfect if you are driving in the woods to go for a hike. It is my favorite of his songs sonically, and overall, it just brings so much joy. Even though the lyrics are not happy, the beat just feels like rolling the windows down on the highway. With Hozier's contribution on it, it makes it one of my favorite songs.
