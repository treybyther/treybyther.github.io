Album Thoughts

As a follow up to Cape Eliabeth, this album you can tell Kahan has settled into his sound, much of which you will hear on his current album. Part of Me is a really strong opener track, where he combines sad lyrics with an upbeat, folk song. His album explores a piano ballad in Fear of Water, and overall he comes out with his strongest album.

Favorite Song

Fear Of Water

As a Noah Kahan song, I wouldn't reccomend this to someone getting into his music. However, as a Kahan fan, I am a sucker for a piano ballad, and it is just so hauntingly beautiful, and actually brings me to tears everytime that I listen to it. If you are looking to be sad, go for this one. Otherwise, I highly reccomend Part of Me.
