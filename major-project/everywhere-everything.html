Reasoning

I really love this song as well, with and without the feature. The feature is Gracie Abrams, who is another one of my all time favorite artists. Overall, I think that this one is a heartfelt song that I really enjoy listening to when I don't want to listen to his sad music. This is an upbeat song, with an upbeat message, that he wants to be with someone until he decomposes. It's a sweet song, and it's very catchy with great instrumentals. And Abrams's vocals are very beautiful on here.
