Facts

Album release: June 14, 2019

Run time: 35 minutes, 28 seconds

Album Thoughts

As a follow up to his first EP, and his first full length studio album, this is what sets Kahan apart from other artists in my opinion. He is able to create a coherent album, while keeping it fresh with each song. False Confidence, into Mess, is a really strong introduction to the album, and I would highly reccomend this album to anyone trying to get into Noah Kahan.

Favorite Song

Carlo's Song

I was immediately drawn to this album closer, because it's a song he is singing to a "you." I love this concept, because it allows everyone to put themselves in the shoes of singing to someone, when singing along or listening. It also musically is the strongest mix of slower beats mixed with an upbeat chorus. It almost made my overall favorite song list.
