Album Thoughts

For a debut EP, Kahan creates a strong name for himself, establishing not only a strong and unique tone, but a very recognizable sound for himself. A country, indie, folk fusion, with a bigger name like Julia Michaels featured on the album.

Favorite Song

Catastrophize

This is my favorite song because I think it has a catchy, upbeat, and addicting instrumentation. I also like how it takes a word like "catastrophize" and makes it into a catchy chorus, which you wouldn't immediately think when looking at a word like that. It's a standout on the album in terms of catchiness and rememberability.
